title: "MTG News for 2025-03-06 - Hasbro’s new and expanding “Magic: The Gathering” ..."
date: 2025-03-06
---

Hasbro’s new and expanding “Magic: The Gathering” movie and TV movie universe to include an adaptation from Legendary Entertainment’s “Godzilla” franchise, which is set to be directed by Dean Devlin (“The King’s Speech,” “American Sniper”), and a non-Magic: The Gathering-based movie from Hasbro’s Dune franchise. The new project will be produced by Exec Producer Jon Jashni and executive produced by Jeff Robinov.
